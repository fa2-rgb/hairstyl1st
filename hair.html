Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our services.

Our salon is open Tuesday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on Mondays.

While walk-ins are welcome if we have availability, we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure you get your preferred time and stylist. You can book online or call us.

We kindly request at least 24 hours notice for any cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee.

We use a curated selection of high-quality, professional hair care products, including options that are sulfate-free, paraben-free, and cruelty-free. Our stylists can recommend the best products for your hair type and needs.
